Welcome to CSST 2025 Summer Institute

The Consortium for the Science of Sociotechnical Systems (CSST) Summer Institute is a career development workshop bringing together emerging scholars in sociotechnical research. This intensive 5-day program pairs 21 mentees with 7 experienced mentors for deep engagement around the challenges and opportunities of interdisciplinary research careers.

Event Details

  • Dates: May 28 - June 1, 2025
  • Location: Minnowbrook Conference Center, Indian Lake, NY
  • Format: Intensive workshops, mentoring sessions, and collaborative activities
  • Focus: Navigating interdisciplinary careers in sociotechnical research
